Method & caveats

  • Disposal floor — the projection minus a margin of safety (z75% × the player's recent std-dev), so erratic players are discounted more. Under a normal approximation they clear it in ~75% of games.
  • Goal floor (hero) — the largest k with P(≥k)≥65% under Poisson(λ=projection), shown as k+ goals; the cell is highlighted when the floor backs 1+ goal at 65%. 1+ rate is a supporting figure — the separate empirical share of recent games with a goal.
  • Projection blend (backtest-tuned, season-anchored) — recent form is split across three windows (L3/L5/L10). With H2H: 0.65·season + 0.15·L3 + 0.05·L5 + 0.05·L10 + 0.10·H2H, without: 0.55·season + 0.15·L3 + 0.05·L5 + 0.25·L10; H2H is recency-weighted (2026 counts 3× a 2024 meeting).
  • Top 10 players per club by game-time then disposal projection. Floors use current-season games (recent games across seasons if too few).
